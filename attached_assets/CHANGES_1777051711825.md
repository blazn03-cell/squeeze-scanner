## New: VERDICT engine

Every card now ends with an explicit verdict banner matching your framework memory rule:

- Bullish tech + Bullish macro → **CALLS** with confidence %
- Bearish tech + Bearish macro → **PUTS** with confidence %
- Any conflict (tech vs macro) → **CASH** with explanation
- Neutral anywhere → **CASH**

The verdict chip also shows on the **collapsed row** so you see CALLS / PUTS / CASH at a glance without expanding.

Function: `getTechnicalRead(p, macroBias)` at ~line 210. `macroBias` is derived from the market tide (macro_bull / macro_bear signals in the feed) and piped down App → Column → Card.

## What I deliberately did NOT build

**Full Ichimoku / TTM Squeeze / StochasticMACD math.** That needs OHLC history fetches per ticker — blows the 60s scan budget and doubles UW API cost. What your VZ chart actually shows you as the bottom-line read is the header: `Bias: Bearish · IV Rank: 50% · Suggested: Credit Spread`. That's what the scanner now gives you per ticker, derived from UW's GEX + IV + flow data.

**If you want full Ichimoku math added as an on-demand "deep read" button** for a single ticker at a time (fetches OHLC, computes cloud/Tenkan/Kijun/Span A/Span B, returns a read) — say the word and I'll wire it up. It'd be a button on the expanded card, not part of the bulk scan.
